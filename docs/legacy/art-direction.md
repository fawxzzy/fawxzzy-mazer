## Composition
- Scene centers a square board/maze panel in the viewport.
- Board reads as a framed translucent slab over a full-screen cosmic backdrop.
- Main menu controls sit low, left/center/right, outside the board frame.

## Required visual motifs
- **Purple starfield background**
  - deep violet/indigo space texture with bright scattered stars.
- **Centered square board**
  - near-symmetric, fixed-aspect square occupying middle third-to-half of screen.
- **Translucent green title**
  - “Mazer” wordmark in neon green with low-opacity/glass feel.
- **Grayscale maze**
  - walls/floors use dark charcoal to light gray values with hard pixel-like edges.
- **Cyan trail**
  - player traversal path is highlighted in bright cyan/blue.
- **Red goal marker**
  - end objective appears as small red marker near path terminus.
- **Minimal retro menu structure**
  - compact rectangular button outlines, sparse typography, no dense chrome.

## Tone + rendering guidance
- Keep board readable first; decorative effects remain secondary.
- Favor crisp orthographic/pixel-ish line work over soft bevel-heavy UI.
- Preserve high contrast between neutral maze values and accent colors (green/cyan/red).
